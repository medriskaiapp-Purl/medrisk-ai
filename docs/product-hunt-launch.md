# Product Hunt Launch Guide

## Why Product Hunt

- 5M+ monthly visitors, many are B2B buyers
- Free to launch
- No personal brand needed (launch as "MedRisk AI")
- One-time spike + permanent archive (people find it later via Google)
- Top 5 product of the day = thousands of visitors

---

## Preparation (1-2 days before launch)

### 1. Create Maker Account

1. Go to producthunt.com
2. Sign up with new email
3. Profile name: "MedRisk AI" (or a pseudonym)
4. No personal photo needed — use the MedRisk AI logo

### 2. Prepare Materials

**Tagline (60 chars max):**
"AI-powered ISO 14971 risk analysis in 10 minutes"

**Description (260 chars):**
"Describe your medical device. Get an audit-ready risk analysis with physics-based controls, IEC 60601 clause mapping, and quantitative verification criteria. Free to try. Replaces months of consultant work."

**Screenshots (4-5):**
1. Main app screen with device input form
2. Generated risk analysis (show Risk 1-2 with full detail)
3. Risk summary table
4. Pricing comparison (consultant vs MedRisk AI)
5. Sample PDF output

**First Comment (write this — it shows when people click):**
```
Hi Product Hunt!

I built MedRisk AI because I've seen firsthand how painful ISO 14971 risk
analysis is for medical device startups. Consultants charge $15-50K and take
months. Small teams skip it or do it poorly.

MedRisk AI generates audit-ready risk documentation in 10 minutes:
- Physics-based risk controls (not generic checkbox compliance)
- IEC 60601 clause mapping for every risk
- Quantitative verification criteria (specific instruments, pass/fail values)
- Defense-in-depth: design + protective + information controls

Try the free tier (5 risks) — no signup needed. Pro reports ($99) include
up to 25 risks with PDF download.

I'd love your feedback, especially if you work in medical devices or regulatory.
What device would you want to analyze?
```

### 3. Pick Launch Day

- **Best days:** Tuesday, Wednesday, Thursday
- **Best time:** 00:01 AM Pacific (midnight) — Product Hunt resets daily at midnight PT
- **Avoid:** Monday (catching up from weekend), Friday (low traffic)

---

## Launch Day

1. Submit product at 00:01 AM Pacific
2. Post first comment immediately
3. Share the Product Hunt link on:
   - Reddit (r/medicaldevices, r/SaaS, r/startups)
   - Indie Hackers
   - Any relevant communities
4. Respond to EVERY comment within 1 hour
5. Be genuine — answer questions, take feedback

## After Launch

- Reply to all comments within 24 hours
- If you get top 5: update landing page with "Featured on Product Hunt" badge
- Product Hunt page stays forever — people find it via Google months later
- Check analytics: how many clicked through to your app?
